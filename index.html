more about me

Hi there, I'm Bridget.

I like to describe myself as a Naija-born, Ukraine-bred avid lover of hats - literally and figuratively. Over the past years, I worn a few professional hats: as a trained construction engineer, project manager, educator, client relations executive, and now as an enthusiastic creative designer.

I apply the extensive knowledge and experience that I've gained from prior fields into creating deigns that are empathetic, user-friendly, fluid and innovative.

I'm currently based in Southern Nigeria, rediscovering the country's culture and cuisine after over a decade of living in Ukraine, and working remotely with clients from all around the world.
